Hero Setup

Input the Star Level, Awaken, Weapon Level and Medals for each of your heroes. Remember to save your changes!

Input Information and Validation

Each field expects integer inputs. Mischievous behaviour and unintended inputs are controlled to some extent on the client-side through scripts and standard attributes. These are utilized to ensure valid parameters. Once submitted a server-side validation will process the input per below.

Star Level and Awaken allows one digit 0 - 7. Inevitably a hero's Awaken cannot be greater than its Star Level. Should this be submitted the server-side will decrease the Awaken to match the respective Star Level. Leave at 0 for heroes you have not yet unlocked.

Weapon Level allows one digit 0 - 9. Input smaller than 0 or greater than 9 will revert the level to its previous value. Weapon level is used to determine unlocked Rune Slots.

Medals allows four digits and the server-side validation will adjust the value to never surpass the total medals of a 7-star hero.

Invalid inputs will result in an error and no changes will be saved.
