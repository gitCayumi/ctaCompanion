Boss Teams

Calculate your optimal damage teams for each boss in the Slay Boss Event.

Recent findings within the community show that some heroes deal more or less damage than they're supposed to using their in-game data. This is currently being investigated and the base values used by this web application will be updated accordingly.

It's still safe to use this algorithm to generate your teams but their damage relations may vary greater in-game than you see here.

Also keep in mind the nature of RNG in-game with frenzy, critical strikes and spawn order.

The algorithm adds one hero at a time selecting the one who has the greatest impact on a teams combined damage. This is also the order in which heroes are sorted within each boss. Please allow a few seconds for the algorithm to run.
