Quickstart Guide v1

This guide is for players just starting out, pointing you in the right direction and possibly answering a few questions you might have.

Crush Them All is all about collecting heroes, making them stronger and using them in various game modes. The game modes can be broken down into two categories; PVE (Player vs Environment) and PVP (Player vs Player). Some game modes are played on your own, while others are played together with your guild.
